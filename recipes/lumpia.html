What is Lumpia

Lumpia Shanghai is staple filipino dish that's good for any special occasion! It's made with a mixture of pork, beef, carrots, and green beans.

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork
  • Ground Beef
  • Ground Carrots
  • Ground Green Beans

Steps

  1. Cook your Ground Beef and Ground Pork and drain it off all its liquids and fats
  2. Grind up some carrots and green beans
  3. Mix together well
  4. Spread out egg roll wrappers
  5. Add filling to wgg roll wrapper leaving enough space for folding
  6. Seal edges with light brushing of water
  7. After wrapping is finished fry lumpia until golden brown
